Friday Night Socials

Welcome to Bangalore's electrifying Friday Night Socials, the city's most happening social event where the energy is contagious, and the vibes are simply irresistible. Held at the trendiest venues in town, Friday Night Socials is a one-of-a-kind experience that brings together people from all walks of life to celebrate the end of the workweek and welcome the weekend in style. As the sun sets over the city, Bangalore comes alive with an electric atmosphere, and Friday Night Socials is at the heart of it all. Whether you're a local resident looking to unwind after a long week or a visitor seeking to experience the city's vibrant nightlife, this event promises an unforgettable evening. Expect an impressive lineup of top-notch DJs and live bands, delivering a mix of pulsating beats and chart-topping hits that will keep you on the dance floor all night long. From pulsating EDM to groovy hip-hop and soulful retro tracks, the music caters to diverse tastes, ensuring everyone finds their rhythm.
