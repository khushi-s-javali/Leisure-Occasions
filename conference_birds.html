The Conference of The Birds

Welcome to our Drama Event, where the stage comes alive with passion, intensity, and the raw emotions of human life. Step into a world of captivating narratives, riveting performances, and the sheer power of the theatrical arts. Our Drama Event is a celebration of the art of storytelling through live performance. Be prepared to embark on a journey that traverses the depths of human experiences, as our talented actors bring characters to life and breathe soul into unforgettable stories. In the intimate setting of our theater, stories will unfold that resonate with the heart and mind. The dramatic tales presented on stage will transport you to different worlds and eras, exploring the complexities of the human condition and the universal themes that connect us all.

Location: Girinagar

Price: 500/-

Date: 31st August

Timings: 6:00PM to 10:00PM

Don't miss the opportunity to be a part of this captivating theatrical experience. Secure your seats early, and be prepared to be transported to a world of emotions, imagination, and thought-provoking drama.
